Our Mission and Values

At SAS, we are committed to supporting and advocating for society's most vulnerable members. Our mission is to provide assistance to those in need, and to create a stronger, more resilient community. We believe in the following core values:

  • Compassion - we approach our work with empathy and kindness towards others.
  • Inclusivity - we strive to create a welcoming and supportive environment for all individuals.
  • Collaboration - we partner with local businesses, government agencies, and other non-profit organizations to create a comprehensive network of support.
  • Integrity - we maintain the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior.
  • Innovation - we seek out new and creative solutions to the challenges facing our community.

Our History

SAS was founded in 2010 by a group of passionate individuals who saw a need in the community for greater support of vulnerable populations. Over the years, our organization has grown and evolved to meet the changing needs of our clients.

Today, we offer a variety of programs that address issues such as housing insecurity, unemployment, and lack of access to healthcare. We work with a wide range of partners to provide comprehensive support to our clients, including job training, medical referrals, and transportation assistance.

We are proud of our organization's history and the impact we have made on the lives of our clients. We look forward to continuing to serve our community and make a positive difference in the world.
